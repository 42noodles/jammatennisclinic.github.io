Our Mission Statement

Created by High School Students, JAMMA’s mission statement is to spread our love for tennis across the
nation by providing affordable and accessible tennis lessons in a safe environment to honor our retired
coach’s impact on us and giving back to causes we are passionate about.

Safe Environment

To keep the lessons as safe as possible, groups will be limited to 5-6 kids, where we will maintain at least 6 feet of social distancing for the entire lesson and wear masks. For more information on the guidelines we will follow, click on this link!

Good Cause

All our proceeds are donated to the Michael J. Fox Organization to fuel the fight against Parkinson's Disease. (We charge $20/hour)

Experienced Teachers

At JAMMA, all instructors have played for 7+ years, and play high up in their respective highschool varsity teams and can help your child find success in their tennis careers.
