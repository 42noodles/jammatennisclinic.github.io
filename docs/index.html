Who are we?

We're a group of high school students who we were brought together by an amazing coach named Francois
when we were growing up. Francois has had a tremendous impact on our lives, and in honor of this impact he's had on us,
we're putting on weekly Saturday tennis clinics from 3-4pm at _____ just like he did when we were young.
We hope to spread the love for the sport that he instilled in us, especially during the challenging times we're in right now.

Safe Environment

To meet social distancing protocols, groups will be limited to 5-6 kids, where we will maintain at least 6 feet of social distancing for the entire lesson.

Free Teaching

Our main priority is to give back to the community during these trying times and to honor Francois's impact on us. Because of this, we charge nothing.

Skilled Teachers

At JAMMA, all instructors have played for 7+ years, and play high up in their respective highschool varsity teams.
